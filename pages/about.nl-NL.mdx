# About WinUI Blog

WinUI Blog was created by [Kilian Balaguer](https://github.com/kilianbalaguer) in 2024. The docs focuses on delivering insightful content about the WinUI framework, UI/UX design principles, and the latest trends in front-end development.

## Team

The project is currently maintained by its creator, [Kilian Balaguer](https://github.com/kilianbalaguer).

## Mission

WinUI Blog aims to be a leading resource for developers and designers interested in mastering the WinUI framework. Our mission is to provide high-quality tutorials, best practices, and news to help our readers stay ahead in the fast-evolving world of UI development.
